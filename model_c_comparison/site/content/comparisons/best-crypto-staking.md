        ## ‚ö†Ô∏è Staking Risks to Know
        
        ### 1. Lock-Up Periods
        Some staking requires 30-90 day lock. Can't access funds if price crashes!

        
        ### 2. Slashing Risk
        On-chain staking (ETH, ATOM) can lose coins if validator misbehaves. Exchange staking protects you.

        
        ### 3. Opportunity Cost
        5% staking APY < 50% price movement! Consider if better to trade vs stake.

        
        ### 4. Exchange Risk
        Your coins are on exchange, not your wallet. Choose secure platforms (Kraken = best track record).
